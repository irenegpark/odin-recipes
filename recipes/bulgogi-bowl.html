Bulgogi Bowl

The Bulgogi Bowl is a simple recipe. Bulgogi is usually marinated thin shaved slices of beef. This dish, however, uses ground beef. The whole recipe is anxiety-free and takes only a few minutes. You will feel like you are eating your Korean mother's home cooked meal.

    Ingredients
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seed oil
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup water
  • minced garlic, to taste
  • minced ginger
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 cup cooked brown rice
  • 1 head romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1 cucumber, diced
    Steps
  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes. Drain and discard grease. Mix in sesame oil.
  2. Combine soy sauce, water, garlic, ginger, red pepper flakes, and black pepper in a food processor. Blend until combined but still slightly chunky. Pour mixture into the skillet with the beef and cook over medium heat until most of the liquid evaporates, 7 to 9 minutes.
  3. Place 1/4 the cooked brown rice in the bottom of an individual serving bowl. Top with a portion of beef mixture, lettuce, cucumber, and sesame seeds. Repeat with remaining rice, beef, and toppings.
