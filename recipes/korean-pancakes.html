Korean Pancakes

Korean Pancakes, sometimes called Korean Pizza, is not like American pancakes. Korean pancakes are savory and is a good dish to get rid of leftovers. My favorite Korean Pancakes usually have pieces of kimchee, onions, and imitation crab.

    Ingredients
  • 1 cup Korean-pancake mix
  • 1 cup kimchee. sliced
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 onion, small, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seed oil
  • 1 pinch red pepper flakes
    Steps
  1. Preheat a griddle over medium heat.
  2. Combine korean-pancake mix, kimchee, water, egg, and onions in a bowl.
  3. Scoop about 1/3 cup batter onto hot griddle. Cook until browned, 3 to 4 minutes per side. Repeat with the remaining batter.
  4. Combine soy sauce, vinegar, sesame oil, garlic, and red pepper flakes together in a bowl; serve sauce alongside pancakes.
